Maximum annual temperatures at Moorabbin Airport, Melbourne
-----------------------------------------------------------

Description
~~~~~~~~~~~

Maximum annual temperatures (degrees Celsius) for Moorabbin Airport,
Melbourne. 1971-2016.

Format
~~~~~~

Annual time series of class ``ts``.

Source
~~~~~~

Australian Bureau of Meteorology.

Examples
~~~~~~~~

::


   autoplot(maxtemp)
