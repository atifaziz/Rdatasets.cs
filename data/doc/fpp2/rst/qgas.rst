Quarterly Australian Gas Production
-----------------------------------

Description
~~~~~~~~~~~

Total quarterly gas production in Australia (in petajoules) from 1956:Q1
to 2010:Q2.

Format
~~~~~~

Quarterly time series of class ``ts``.

Source
~~~~~~

Australian Bureau of Statistics. Cat. 8301.0.55.001.

Examples
~~~~~~~~

::


   autoplot(qgas)
